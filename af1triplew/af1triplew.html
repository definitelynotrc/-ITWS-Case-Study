Nike Air Force 1 'Triple white'

Men's Shoe

The Nike Air Force 1 Triple White features triple white leather upper perforations on the toebox as well as two perforations under each Swoosh. The Swoosh and overlays are also white leather. The outsole is white with a concentric-circles pattern and stars in the rubber. The midsole is white with a hidden Air compartment. The midsole says "AIR" in white. A silver tab saying "AF1" is on the top of the white laces.
