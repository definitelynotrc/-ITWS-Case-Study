Nike Air Force 1 'Travis Scott Sail'

Men's Shoe

With Astroworld upon us, its about time Cactus Jack blessed us with a new pair of kicks too. This time, Nike boy Travis Scott linked up with the Swoosh on another colorway of the Air Force 1 Low silhouette. With nearly identical features as its predecessor, this model features a sail canvas upper with reinforced stitching. Naturally though, the most eye catching features on this pair are the chromed out Nike Swoosh on both the lateral and medial sides and the Cactus Jack patch on top of the laces. This pair is finished off with a clean gum bottom.
